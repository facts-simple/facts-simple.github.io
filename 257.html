Multiple sunrises

2016-12-28

Fact Source(s):
  • Heavens Above
As the multiple sunrises and sunsets would confuse the body's biological clock, the windows are covered to give the impression of darkness. The space station conforms to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC), waking at 06:00 and sleeping from 21:30.
