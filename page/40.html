Strong bass notes

2016-12-07

Fact Source(s):
  • Jordan Kirkness
September is Classical Music Month. Despite having a frequency range of 40 to 100Hz, all the strings are tightened to a similar tension, which means that the gauge of the string adjusts the frequency and not the tension.

A violin is made of...

2016-12-05

Fact Source(s):
  • The Violin Site
September is Classical Music Month. Usually Maple makes up the back, neck, ribs and scroll; Spruce, the top, linings and blocks and Ebony, the pegs, fingerboard, end pin and tailpiece.

The oldest orchestra in the world

2016-12-05

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Royal Danish Orchestra)
September is Classical Music Month. Although not an orchestra in the modern sense, it originated as an ensemble of trumpeters for the King's Court. ​In Danish their name means The King's Chapel (Det Kongelige Kapel).
