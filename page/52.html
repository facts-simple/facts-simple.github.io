The oldest concert venue in the world

2016-12-03

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Odeon of Herodes Atticus)
September is Classical Music Month. The Odeon had a roof when it was first built, even then it wasn't quite a concert 'hall', as such; but it did host musical events in the early years. The roof was destroyed in 267 A.D. and the theatre lay in ruin until its restoration in 1950. Since then has hosted many concerts both classical and contemporary. The Odeon of Herodes Atticus is  on the same hill as the Acropolis of Athens, Greece.
