Woolly pianos

2016-12-10

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Ivory)
September is Classical Music Month. For at least 300 years, dead mammoth ivory has been legally traded. However it is expensive, naturally, as there are limited numbers of mammoth remains in the world. Some suggest that a few hundred million could still remain untouched in Siberia.

The perfect alien communicator?

2016-12-10

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Teen Age Message)
September is Classical Music Month. The theremin was considered the best instrument to go with the coded transmission. Three theremin players from Moscow played the pieces for transmission. It was the first time a song was broadcast into space to reach extraterrestrial life. It was broadcast by the Russian Institute of Radio-engineering and Electronics. Seven years later, NASA broadcast 'Across the Universe' by The Beatles.
