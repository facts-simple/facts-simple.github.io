Your daily water footprint

2016-10-14

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Water use)
The figure seems incredibly high, and what should be noted is that it is the the indirect use of freshwater. Many consumables, such as food and other commodities require large amounts of water in their production. Even nuclear power plants use enormous quantities of water to cool the reactors and generate electricity from steam.
