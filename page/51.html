Louis XIX, shortest-reigning monarch

2016-10-12

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (List of shortest-reigning monarchs)
Although he was never crowned, he was the de facto King of France on the 2nd of August 1830 because he had not made his abdication official by signing the papers. His reign is often disputed as being more philosophical than lawful however. The shortest reign of a monarch who was crowned was Emperor Mo, who reigned for 12 hours after his coronation, before being killed in the Siege of Caizhou (9th February 1234).
