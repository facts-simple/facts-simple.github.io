How Cumberbatch played the violin

2016-12-02

Fact Source(s):
  • The Guardian
September is Classical Music Month. The professional violinist was Eos Counsell, a member of the Bond string quartet and a holder of a Honours Degree from the Royal College of Music. Although credit where it's due to Cumberbatch, he was coached how to play the violin like an expert, or at least, appear to on camera by Counsell. He was coached for a few weeks to perfect the movement of the bow, stance and hold.
