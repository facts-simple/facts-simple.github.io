19th September, 2016 Science

The earliest recording of a musical performance

18th September, 2016 Music

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Phonograph cylinder)
September is Classical Music Month. The recording was made on a Phonograph, the first practical recording and playback device. The vibrations are recorded onto a wax cylinder which can be used to play the sounds back. Recordings of voice and humming were made prior, but this is one of the earliest surviving recordings of a musical performance. *He is not pictured.
