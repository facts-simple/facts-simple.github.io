Blue water

10th January, 2017 Science

Fact Source(s):
  • Wikipedia (Water)
The colour is not usually seen in its liquid form, when the sea is perceived as blue it is only a reflection of the sky. In thicknesses of 10m or more the colour is more visible.

Gas guzzler

9th January, 2017 Transportation

Fact Source(s):
  • Concorde SST
If a transatlantic flight was carried out entirely at full power, 10,000 gallons would be expended over the course of an average of 3.5 hours. Despite its massive environmental impact, Concorde remains the fastest transportation method of passengers ever. For those ignorant of, or too young to know the plane's history, the last commercial flights were carried out in 2003. None has flown since.
